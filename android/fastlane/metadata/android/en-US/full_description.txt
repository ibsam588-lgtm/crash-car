Crash Car is a fast arcade driving game where speed, impact, and combo timing decide your score.

Choose a realistic muscle car, race through a construction yard, and smash through crates, barrels, cones, and barricades. Chain crashes to grow your combo, earn coins, unlock new cars, and upgrade speed, handling, and durability for bigger runs.

Features:
- Realistic generated car art with arcade crash gameplay
- Flame-powered collision, debris, speed, combo, and damage systems
- Garage with unlockable cars and upgrades
- Score summary with coins, max speed, objects hit, damage, and best combo
- Shop-ready screens for rewarded ads, coin packs, premium cars, and ad removal
