Crash Car is a fast arcade driving game where speed, impact, and combo timing decide your score.

Choose a realistic muscle car, race through destructible arenas, or launch off a ramp into a busy city intersection. Hit cars, trucks, buses, shops, and barriers, then keep the crash chain alive as vehicles skid sideways, break apart, and score in slow motion.

Earn coins, unlock new cars, and upgrade speed, handling, and durability for bigger runs.

Features:
- Two play methods: Classic Arena and Ramp Launch
- Realistic generated cars, trucks, buses, wrecks, and traffic
- Flame-powered collision, debris, speed, combo, slow-motion, and damage systems
- Ramp setup with arena, car, and charge-time selection
- Garage with unlockable cars and upgrades
- Score summary with coins, max speed, objects hit, damage, and best combo
- Shop-ready screens for rewarded ads, coin packs, premium cars, and ad removal
